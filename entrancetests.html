JEE Main

Joint Entrance Examination Main (JEE Main) is a national-level entrance test conducted for admission to various undergraduate engineering programs in prestigious institutions across India.

JEE Advanced

JEE Advanced is the second phase of the Joint Entrance Examination, offering admissions to the Indian Institutes of Technology (IITs) and other top engineering colleges in India.

BITSAT

Birla Institute of Technology and Science Admission Test) is a computer-based entrance exam conducted by BITS Pilani for admissions to its integrated first-degree programs, including engineering, at its campuses in Pilani, Goa, and Hyderabad.


MET

Manipal Entrance Test is a university-level entrance exam conducted by Manipal Academy of Higher Education (MAHE) for admissions to various undergraduate and postgraduate programs.

KCET

Karnataka Common Entrance Test (KCET) is a state-level entrance examination conducted by the Karnataka Examination Authority (KEA) for admission to various undergraduate courses in colleges across Karnataka.

COMEDK

Consortium of Medical, Engineering and Dental Colleges of Karnataka is an entrance exam conducted for admissions to various engineering and medical courses in private colleges in Karnataka.


VITEEE

VIT Engineering Entrance Examination is a computer-based test conducted by VIT University for admissions to its B.Tech programs. It assesses students' knowledge in various subjects like Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, and English.

SRMJEEE

SRM Joint Engineering Entrance Examination is a national-level entrance exam conducted by SRM Institute of Science and Technology for admissions to its various engineering programs. The exam evaluates students' knowledge in subjects like Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, and critical thinking abilities.

AEEE

Amrita Entrance Examination - Engineering is a prestigious entrance test for admission to the engineering programs offered by Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham. It assesses the candidates' skills in Mathematics, Physics, and Chemistry.
